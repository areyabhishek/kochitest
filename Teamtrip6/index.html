What is Culture-First Organizations?

Culture-first organizations are companies that prioritize and value their workplace culture above all else. They focus on creating a positive and inclusive work environment where employees feel valued, engaged, and motivated. These organizations often have strong core values that guide their decisions and actions, and they invest time and resources in building a supportive culture that aligns with their values.
